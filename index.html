Our Story

From Bihar's Ponds to Your Doorstep

We started Jubilee Makhana with one simple mission — bring the healthiest snack on earth, directly from source, to every home in India.

Makhana has been grown in the ponds of Bihar for centuries — a superfood that's naturally high in protein, low in calories, and packed with antioxidants. We partner directly with farmers in Darbhanga and Madhubani, ensuring fair wages, zero middlemen, and the freshest possible product. Every batch is hand-sorted, slow-roasted (never fried), and lovingly seasoned.
